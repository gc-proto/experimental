Canada.ca AI Answers demo pages

These test.canada.ca pages demonstrate how the AI Answers banner can be added to a page, and how that page context comes along as a URL when the user clicks through to use AI Answers. The 'Page you were on' URL is displayed to the user on the AI Answers page — this may help them understand they can ask a more detailed question. The referring URL is available as context to the AI pipeline in the search and institution-context selection stages. It also is available during the Answer stage as context for the AI, and is often downloaded and read by the AI as context.

In the future, AI Answers can be built into the canada.ca infrastructure, rather than added on a per-page or per-section basis. The AI Answers banner can be customized and implemented in AEM and non-AEM sites, see the Implementation instructions to learn more.

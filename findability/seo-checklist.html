Page title
Page title

The metadata title tag (<title></title>) contains the text that will be displayed in a search engine result. Title tags are also used in browser history, bookmarks, and social media.

The <h1></h1> tag is title that users see when they are on a web page.

Aim for as concise titles as possible, since very long titles may be truncated in search results especially on mobile.

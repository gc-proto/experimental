National Online Licensing System:

The National Online Licensing System applies to holders of fishing licences managed by Fisheries and Oceans Canada (DFO), including:

  • holders of commercial licences
  • holders of commercial-communal licences
  • holders of recreational fishing licences in Eastern Canada (Atlantic Canada and Quebec)
  • International fishers and registered fish harvesters (including crew members)

With your account, you can:

  • pay fees
  • submit licence requests
  • print your licences
  • appoint a representative
  • appoint an individual to do business on behalf of your organization

Help with your account

Forgot your GCKey password or username

  • first select Sign in to your account
  • Then select Sign-in with GCkey
  • then select Forgot your username or Forgot your password

Use a different Sign-in partner

If you've moved to a new bank or credit union, and already sign in through Sign-in Partner, you may be able to switch:

  • first select Sign in to your account
  • then select Sign-in Partner
  • then select Switch My Sign-in Partner from the menu bar

Lost your DFO passcode

Request a new DFO Passcode if you lost your Passcode, your Passcode expired, or you changed your GCKey username and password. If you make changes to your Sign-In Partner information you will need your Passcode reset.

To request a new DFO Passcode, contact Client Support.

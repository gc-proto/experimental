Help with GCKey

Use your GCKey username and password to sign in to most Government of Canada online accounts and services. Anyone in any country can sign up and create a GCKey username and password to access online services.

How to sign up for a GCKey username and password

Once you sign up to create a GCKey username and password, you can use it to sign in to any Government of Canada online account that supports GCKey.

You still have to register for each account, so that it can match your GCKey username and password to your account profile.

To sign up:

  • first find the account or government service you want to register for
  • then use the GCKey button to register or sign-in, if GCKey is supported for that account
  • on the Welcome to GCKey page, click the Sign up button
Whenever you sign in to that account in the future, use the GCKey username and password to sign in.

Anyone in any country can use the GCKey service provided by the Government of Canada.

Check if you need another GCKey username and password

You usually only need one GCKey username and password to sign into any government service that supports GCKey:

  • for example, sign up for a GCKey username and password to register and reserve a campsite in My Parks Canada Reservation Account
  • then use your same GCKey username and password to register and sign in to My Service Canada Account to manage your Employment Insurance or Canada Pension

You can have more than one GCKey username and password pair, for example for work vs personal accounts. If you do sign up for another:

  • remember which username and password pair to use with each service
  • use a different recovery email address for each GCKey username and password pair

If your GCKey username and password have expired after not being used for 2 years, sign up and start again.

Creating your username and password [CHELSEY – first review done]

Create your username:

You can find rules for creating your username in the Username Checklist box on the Create your username page. You will not be able to proceed unless all requirements listed in that checklist are met.

As you enter a username a red “x” or a green checkmark will appear in the Username Checklist. The “x” means you have not met the username requirements. The checkmark means you have met the username requirements.

Tips for creating a username

When creating your username, you should:

  • make your username easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess
  • avoid using personal information, such as your name, Social Insurance Number (SIN), mailing address, or email address
  • keep your username secure and do not share it with anyone

Each username must be unique. You will receive a message if the username you create has already been used.

Create your password

You can find rules for creating your password in the Password Checklist box. You will not be able to proceed unless all password requirements are met.

As you enter a password a red “x” or a green checkmark will appear in the Password Checklist. The “x” means you have not met the password requirements. The checkmark means you have met the password requirements.

Tips for creating a password

When creating your password, you should:

  • make your password easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess
  • avoid using personal information, such as your name, Social Insurance Number (SIN), mailing address, or email address
  • keep your password secure and do not share it with anyone.

Confirm your password

You must re-enter your password exactly as you entered it into the Create Your Password field. If both passwords do not match, you will not be able to proceed.

Adding your email and recovery questions [CHELSEY – first review done]

Create recovery questions, answers and hints:

Recovery questions are used to help you if you forget your password.

Your questions, answers and hints should be easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess.

Recovery question

Recovery questions and answers are mandatory. To create yours follow these steps:

  1. For the first question, choose a recovery question from the Select a Recovery Question field.
  2. Enter an answer to the question in the My Recovery Answer field.
  3. Next, enter the name of someone you will remember in the Memorable Person field.
    • If you think you may forget the answer to this question, you can enter a hint in the Memorable Person Hint field.
  4. Next, enter a date that you will remember in the My Memorable Date field.
    • If you think you may forget the answer to this question, you can enter a hint in the Memorable Date Hint field.

Add your email address in case you forget username or password

On the Optional Account Recovery page you can add an email address. This email address will be used to help you recover your GCKey account if you forget your username or password.

You do not have to add an email address, this step is optional.

As you enter an email address a red “x” or a green checkmark will appear in the Email Checklist. The “x” means you have not met the email requirements. The checkmark means you have met the email requirements.

Confirmation code:

The confirmation code is the code sent to the recovery email address associated with your GCKey account. It allows you to verify that the email address you entered is correct. Once you have confirmed your email address by entering the confirmation code, the email address can then be used to help you in case you forget your GCkey username or password.

Note: If your email address is associated with another GCKey account, a warning message will appear indicating that your email address is already in use. You will have the option to:

  1. attempt to recover the username and/or password of the other GCKey account associated with that email address by following the process for username recovery or password reset
  2. register a different email address
  3. register the email address to your new GCKey account by completing the email address registration process. If you choose to complete this process, the system will then remove the email address from your other GCKey account

How to sign in

Sign in the same way each time, with the GCKey username and password you used when you registered for the account.

How to sign in with GCKey:

  • On the sign-in page for the account you want to use, select the GCKey button
  • You'll see the Welcome to GCKey page
  • enter the username and password you created when you first signed up for your GCKey
  • click the Sign in button
Forgot your username or password

If you've forgotten your GCKey username or password, you'll need to recover them through the account you want to use:

  • first find the account or online service you plan to use
  • select the GCKey button to sign in to the account you want to use
  • On the Welcome to GCKey page, select Forgot your username or Forgot your password

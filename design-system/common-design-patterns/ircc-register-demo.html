IRCC Secure Account: Register

  1. Gather your documents

    • if you're starting an application to come to Canada, you'll need your personal reference code if you already have one
    • if you've already started an application, have your application number ready
    • if you have an approved study or work permit, have your permit at hand

  2. Choose how you'll register and sign-in

    How do you want to sign in every time you use your account?

    Option 1: Through a new or existing GCKey username and password

    Register through GCKey

    After you select Register through GCKey, select the Sign up button to make a GCKey username and password to use with your account, or enter your GCKey username and password if you already have them.

    Help with GCKey

    What is GCKey?

    • GCKey usernames and passwords are provided by the Government of Canada through the GCKey service
    • You can use your GCKey username and password to register and sign into most Government of Canada accounts or portals
    • you can use the same GCkey username and password for more than one account and on different computers

    Help with GCKey usernames, passwords and recovery questions

    Option 2: Through your Canadian Interac® Sign-in Partner

    Register through your Interac® Sign-in Partner

    Sign in privately with the same ID and password you use online for your Canadian bank or credit union. If your bank or credit union isn't a Sign-in Partner, use a different sign-in option.

    Your banking ID and password are never shared with the Government of Canada. Your government account information is never shared with your bank or credit union.

    Check if your bank or credit union is a Sign-in Partner

    If your bank or credit union isn't on this list, you'll need to register with GCKey.

Your GCKey username and password are missing, invalid or incomplete

If you haven't used your GCKey username and password at least once in the last 2 years, it expired and has been deleted. You'll need to register for the service again and sign up for a new GCKey or choose a different sign-in option. [NOTE check if this is the same for all services?]

Errors when you sign in

403 error - login session expired or invalid

If you see this error message, your browser may have stored old GCKey information. Try one of these three approaches to sign in again - whichever is easiest for you:

  • if you have another browser (e.g. Safari, Google Chrome, etc.), try signing in from there
  • or try from an incognito or private window, so it can't use the old information:
    • use your browser menu to open a New Incognito or New Private window
    • then copy/paste this GCKey IRCC secure account address into the browser address bar: https://onlineservices-servicesenligne-cic.fjgc-gccf.gc.ca/mycic/gccf?lang=eng&idp=gckey&svc=/mycic/start
    • and sign in from the Welcome to GCKey page that opens
  • or clear your browser history, cache and cookies. Note that this could cause you to lose other information stored in your browser like usernames and passwords for remembered sites.

Browser errors - needs update

Update your browser if you see one of these messages when you try to sign in:

  • Page Can’t Be Displayed
  • Cannot Establish a Secure Connection
  • Page Not Available

You can usually find the option to Update your browser in your browser menu, settings or preferences. The latest versions of browsers have security features to keep your personal information safe.

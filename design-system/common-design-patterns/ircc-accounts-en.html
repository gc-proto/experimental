Find an IRCC online account or portal

There are different accounts for various immigration and citizenship applications, paying, checking status and making changes. Before you register or sign in, make sure you're using the right account.

All Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada online accounts and portals

  • IRCC Secure Account to apply to immigrate, for Express Entry, for work and study permits and for some visitor visas
  • Apply for a visitor visa - instructions for your situation
  • Apply for Canadian citizenship online
  • IRCC Transit Visa Portal to apply for a transit visa to travel through Canada in 48 hours or less
  • Employer Portal: International Mobility Program for employers hiring temporary workers who don’t need a Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA)
  • IRCC Designated Learning Institution Portal for institutions to report on the enrolment status of their international students.
  • Authorized Paid Representatives Portal for authorized paid representatives (APR) to complete, pay for, and submit online applications on behalf of their clients
  • IRCC Portal for provinces and territories for recognized representatives to access and nominate candidates in the Express Entry system
  • Provincial and Territorial Public Health Authority Portal for recognized provincial and territorial public health authorities to communicate with Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada electronically

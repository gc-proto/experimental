  2. [MANDATORY] Choose how you'll register and sign-in

    How do you want to sign in every time you use your account?

    Option 1: With a new or existing GCKey username and password

    Register with GCKey

    If you haven't already created a GCKey username and password, use the Sign up button after you select Register with GCKey.

    Help with GCKey
    • sign up and make a GCKey username and password to register and sign into most Government of Canada accounts or portals
    • you can use the same GCkey username and password for different accounts on different computers, phones and web browsers
    • GCKey is not an account. You still have to register for each online account, so it can match your GCKey username and password to your account profile.
    • keep your browser updated

    Help with GCKey usernames, passwords and recovery questions

    Option 2: Through your Canadian Interac® Sign-in Partner

    Register through your Interac® Sign-in Partner

    If you bank online, you may be able to sign in privately through the same ID and password you use for your Canadian bank or credit union. If you don't have a Canadian Sign-in Partner, register through a different sign-in option.

    None of your banking information is shared with the Government of Canada.

    Check if your bank is a Sign-in partner

    If your bank or credit union isn't on this list, you'll need to select one of the other options to register.

    Option 3: Through some provinces

    Register through Alberta or BC

    If you have an online account with Alberta, BC or Quebec, you can register immediately, without needing to verify your identity through an access code or your bank.

    If you live in another province or you don't have online access in Alberta or B.C., register through a different sign-in option, like GCKey username and password.

    Check if you can use your province's digital ID

    If you live in a province other than BC or Alberta, or you don't have online access in Alberta or B.C., register through a different sign-in option.

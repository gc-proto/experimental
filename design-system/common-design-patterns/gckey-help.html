Signing up for a GCKey username and password

GCKey is provided by the Government of Canada so that you can sign with the same username and password to different accounts across government departments and agencies.

You still have to register for each account, so that it can match your GCKey username and password to your account profile.

  • For example, you can sign up for a GCKey username and password to register and reserve a campsite in My Parks Canada Reservation Account
  • then use your same GCKey username and password to register and sign in to My Service Canada Account to manage your Employment Insurance or Canada Pension
  • anyone in any country can sign up and get a GCKey username and password

You usually only need one GCKey username and password.You can also sign up for another GCKey username and password, for example for work vs personal accounts. Make sure to remember which username and password pair to use with each service. You'll also need to use a different recovery email address for each GCKey username and password.

Creating your username and password [CHELSEY]

Create Your Username:

When creating your Username, we recommend that you:

  • make your Username easy for you to remember and hard for others to guess;
  • avoid using personal information, such as your name, Social Insurance Number (SIN), mailing address, or email address;
  • always keep your Username secure and do not share it with anyone.

Rules for Username creation are located in the Username Checklist Box.

As your Username selection is entered, the rules will either reflect a red “x” to indicate you have not met the requirement, or to a green checkmark to indicate the requirement has been met.

You will not be able to proceed unless all rules are met.

The following characters are permitted in your Username:

  • Capital letters (A - Z)
  • Lower-case letters (a - z)
  • French characters (àâæçéèêëîïôœùûüÿÀÂÆÇÉÈÊËÎÏÔŒÙÛÜŸ)
  • Digits (0 - 9)
  • Special characters: $ % ^ : { } ~ ' ? / | & * _ - . ` ( ) [ ] !

Each Username must be unique. You will receive a message if it has already been used.

Adding your email and recovery questions [CHELSEY]

Create Recovery Questions, Answers and Hints:

These are used to assist you when you forget your password.

Your questions, answers and hints should be easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess. You can optionally use hints to assist you with remembering your answers to these questions.

Add your email address in case you forget username or password

The email address is used to assist you when you forget your username or password. This is an optional step, and you can add your recovery email anytime by going to your main GCKey account page and navigating to the Manage Email section.

Confirmation Code:

This is the code sent to the recovery email address associated with your GCKey account. It is used to confirm your email for username and password recovery.

Note: If your email address is associated with another GCKey account, a warning message will appear indicating that it is already in use. You have the option to 1) attempt to recover the username and/or password of the other GCKey account by following the process for username recovery or password reset, 2) register a different email address or 3) register the email address to your new GCKey account by completing the email address registration process; completing this process will remove the email address from the other GCKey account

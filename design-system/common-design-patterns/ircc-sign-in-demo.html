IRCC Secure Account: Sign in

There are different accounts for various immigration and citizenship application forms, paying, checking status and making changes. Before you register or sign in, check when to use this secure account.

When to use this secure account

Use this account to:

  • start an application for work and study permits, Express Entry, and International Experience Canada
    • you will need your personal reference code if you already have one
    • you can also submit and pay for your application, get messages related to your application, check the status of your application and update your information
    • start an application to extend your stay
  • if you're already a citizen: apply for a proof of citizenship certificate or search for your citizenship record
  • apply for the Interim Federal Health Program if you are a protected person or refugee claimant
  • transfer schools if you are an approved study permit holder
    • you will need your application number
  • apply for some types of visitor visas - get visitor visa instructions for your situation

Don't remember which option you registered with?

You need to sign in the same way every time you use your account.

  • when you first registered for an account, you chose a sign-in option such as GCKey, your banking Sign-in Partner or your province
  • the sign-in option you chose was tied with your account and user profile. Use that same option every time you sign in
  • If you can't remember, try the option that you think you used - if the pages that appear are for registering a new account, return to this page and try the next most likely option

Security steps to protect your account

  • Provide an email address

    All IRCC secure account users will need to have an email address on file to help protect their online accounts from fraudulent activity. If you do not currently have an email address on file, you will be prompted to provide one when you register.

  • Create a set of security questions

    To protect your account from access by someone else, you'll be asked to create a set of personal questions and answers. Keep a private record of your questions and answers so that you can respond with the identical answer (including upper and lower case letters) when asked.

Switching your online banking

If you've moved to a new bank or credit union, and already sign in through Interac® Sign-in Partner, you may be able to use it to sign-in.

  • first, select Interac® Sign-in Partner
  • then select Switch My Sign-in Partner from the menu bar

Your GCKey username and password are missing, invalid or incomplete

If you haven't used your GCKey username and password at least once in the last 2 years, it expired and has been deleted. You'll need to register for the service again and sign up for a new GCKey or choose a different sign-in option. [NOTE check if this is the same for all services?]

Your browser shows an error message

Update your browser

Update your browser if you see one of these messages when you try to sign in:

  • Page Can’t Be Displayed
  • Cannot Establish a Secure Connection
  • Page Not Available

You can usually find the option to Update your browser in your browser menu, settings or preferences. The latest versions of browsers have security features to keep your personal information safe.

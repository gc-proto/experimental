Change your address with the Government of Canada

How to change your address

To keep getting your benefits, payments and services, you must change your address with each government department or agency. Your new address is not shared automatically because government systems aren’t connected.

Follow these steps to update your address:

  • Choose from the common services listed below or use the search tool to find an organization
  • Go to the organization’s website and follow their process to update your address
  • Repeat these steps for every organization that has your address

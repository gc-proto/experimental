Get, renew or replace a status card

How to apply for, renew or replace a Secure Certificate of Indian Status (secure status card) or Certificate of Indian Status (status card).

Possible Canada Post mail delays

Due to Canada Post labour disruption, there may be mailing delays related to registration and secure status card applications. If a labour disruption occurs, applicants who would like to submit their applications for both registration or status cards are encouraged to come in person.

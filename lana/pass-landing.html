Canada Strong Pass

Have the most Canadian summer!

Enjoy free or discounted admissions as you make Canada your travel destination.

The Canada Strong Pass lets you explore the richness of the country — from museums to nature to railroads.

Valid from June 20 to September 2, 2025

  • Completely free - No cost, no purchase required, no sign-up needed
  • No physical pass - Simply show up at participating locations
  • For everyone - All visitors automatically eligible
  • Benefits - Free admission or discounts

Learn more about the pass

Free admission with Parks Canada

Explore national parks, national historic sites and national marine conservation areas.

Discounts on overnight stays with Parks Canada

Enjoy discounted rates on camping, roofed accommodations and historic stays.

Free or reduced admission at museums and galleries

This summer, visit participating museums and galleries across the country. Visit the websites of each participating museum and gallery to find out about free admission and discounts for children and youth.

Free and discounted travel with VIA Rail

Children travel free when accompanied by an adult. Passengers aged 18 to 24 benefit from discounted fares on travel across Canada. Conditions apply.

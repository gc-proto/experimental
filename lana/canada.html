Change of address

  • Update your address with each government organization that provides services to you
  • Address updates are not automatically shared across departments and agencies

Most common service changes

  • Canada Revenue Agency (personal) - personal taxes, tax credits, Canada Child Benefit
  • Canada Revenue Agency (businesses) - business taxes, payroll, GST/HST
  • Service Canada - EI, Canada Pension Plan, Old Age Security
  • Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada - for applications in process
  • Sign up for direct deposit or update your banking information

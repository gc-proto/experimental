Executive Summary

Key Observation

  • The majority of feedback concerns transaction failures rather than information discovery.
  • Authentication issues prevent users from accessing multiple online services.
  • Application status information is frequently unavailable or unclear.
  • Technical failures continue to interrupt document uploads and account transactions.
  • Many users report repeated attempts, suggesting persistent operational issues rather than isolated incidents.

Executive Headline

More than three quarters of all feedback is concentrated in three issues: authentication barriers, inability to access CPP/OAS information, and technical reliability failures.

These issues directly prevent users from accessing services, viewing benefits, checking application status, and completing online transactions.

Executive Action Priorities

  1. Authentication & MFA Stabilization
    Largest source of feedback and blocks service access.
  2. Restore CPP/OAS Visibility
    Improve status, payments, estimates, and confirmation information.
  3. Improve Reliability & Performance
    Address loading issues, upload failures, and error conditions.
  4. Strengthen Self-Service Functions
    Enable easier management of addresses, phones, and banking information.
  5. Clarify MSCA vs CRA Responsibilities
    Reduce user confusion around tax-related information and services.
